About DM Tech

With over two decades of excellence, DM Technologies stands as Tamil Nadu’s leading MSO and a pioneer in Indian cable technology. Our robust infrastructure and state-of-the-art control room ensure an interruption-free, cinema-quality experience that remains unaffected by adverse weather. We are proud to offer the highest number of channels in the market, with over 700 HD and SD channels delivered through our high-performance set-top boxes. Powered by exclusive chipsets, our STBs feature stunning picture quality, surround sound, and advanced functions like USB recording, Pause/Play Live TV, and a unique universal learning remote. Driven by a passion for innovation and a dedicated technical team, DM Tech continues to set the gold standard for digital cable entertainment across South India.
